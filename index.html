How it works
$ mani import commbank-mar-2026.csv
Imported 147 transactions.
Auto-classified 139 of 147 — 8 need review.
$ mani classify --review
Officeworks $89.00 → Office Supplies (100% business)
JB Hi-Fi $349.00 → Equipment (depreciation tracked)
$ mani export --format powerpays --week 2026-W10
Exported 12 claims → powerpays-W10.csv
